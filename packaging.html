Lila's Salón

Brand Identity Design for a modern and inclusive beauty salon.

Concept

Lila's Salón was created as a beauty brand that combines elegance, warmth and inclusivity. Inspired by the idea that beauty enhances confidence, the visual identity uses soft organic forms and a refined color palette to create a welcoming experience.

Visual Identity

The identity system was developed to communicate professionalism, beauty and trust through a minimalist visual language.

Logo Design

A clean logo combined with a floral symbol representing beauty and care.

Color Palette

Warm neutral tones and soft lilac accents create an elegant and welcoming personality.

Typography

Modern typography selected to ensure readability and visual consistency.

Applications

Examples of the visual identity applied across different brand touchpoints.
